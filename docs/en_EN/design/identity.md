## 1. Color Palette

The palette is based on the **"Dark Luxury"** concept. We use a deep dark background to create an atmosphere of premium comfort, with bright gold accents.

### Primary Colors

*   **Deep Emerald:** `#003831`
    *   *Usage:* Main body background, header background, footer.
*   **Royal Gold:** `#EDD071`
    *   *Usage:* Headings, icons, buttons (border/text), active elements, hover effects.

### Secondary Colors

*   **Pure White:** `#FFFFFF`
    *   *Usage:* Main text, card headings.
*   **Muted White:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7)`
    *   *Usage:* Secondary text, descriptions, inactive elements.
*   **Dark Overlay:** `rgba(0, 56, 49, 0.8)`
    *   *Usage:* Dimming background images in cards for text readability.

## 2. Typography

We use a contrasting pair: "Classic" (for headings) + "Modern" (for body text).

### Headings (H1-H6)

*   **Style:** Serif. Elegant, high stroke contrast.
*   **Family:** `Playfair Display`.
*   **Usage:** Block titles, service headings, large price numbers.
*   **Color:** White (`#FFFFFF`) or Gold (`#EDD071`).

### Body Text

*   **Style:** Sans-Serif. Readable, geometric.
*   **Family:** `Lato`.
*   **Usage:** Service descriptions, menu, buttons, price list.

### Accents

*   **Style:** Script (Handwritten).
*   **Family:** `Great Vibes`.
*   **Usage:** Decorative signatures, slogans (e.g., *"Of Your Beauty"*).
